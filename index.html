Black History in Real Time

Because history happens every day

Black History in Real Time was built on one belief: history isn't just February. It's every day, every moment, every story that shaped the world we live in now — and every breakthrough happening right now.

We share daily quizzes and deep dives into Black history they didn't always teach in school, alongside current moments of Black excellence, resistance, and progress happening in real time. This history belongs to everyone.

"Not a lecture. A gift."
